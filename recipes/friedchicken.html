Fried Chicken

Description

The above image showcases how fried chicken looks once it is cooked

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Combine flour and seasonings together to make the dredge inside ziplog bag
  2. Pour buttermilk into bowl, then dip chicken until it is completely soaked in buttermilk
  3. Place chicken inside bag with the dredge and shake to coat it fully
  4. Tranfer coated to baking sheet and allow flour coating to become paste-like consistency
  5. Pour oil into frying pan and bring it to a high heat
  6. Place chicken inside oil and wait till chicken starts browning
  7. Remove chicken using tongs, and place on wiring rack
