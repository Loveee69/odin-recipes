Steak

Description

The above picture contains a picture of a finished ribeye steak

Ingrdients

  • 12oz ribeye steak
  • Seasonings
  • Butter
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Pat steak dry with paper towels to absorb all the moisture
  2. Put a 10 inch skillet on stovetop and pour oil into pan
  3. Season steak with salt and pepper on both sides
  4. Add steak to pan once pan is smoking a bit
  5. Let each side cook for 2 minutes before flipping it
  6. After a flip on each side, bring heat to low and add butter
  7. Once butter is browning, bring heat back to medium and start butter basting
  8. After another 2 minutes, flip steak again, and baste the other side as well
  9. Once the internal temp of the steak is 120 degrees, remove steak and place on cutting board
  10. Let steak rest for 10 minutes before cutting in to allow it to cook fully
  11. Cut it, plate, and serve
