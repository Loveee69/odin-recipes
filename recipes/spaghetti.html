Spaghetti

Description

The image above contains a finished spaghetti dish made from spaghetti and marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Marinara sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fill up large pot full of water, with a pinch of salt, onto the oven to get it boiling
  2. Get another pan and pour marinara sauce into it and keep the heat at a low to simmer
  3. Once water is boiling, add pasta to water and let cook for roughly 10 minutes
  4. Drain the noodles, and place in pan with marinara sauce
  5. Add a bit of pasta water, and stir everything all together
